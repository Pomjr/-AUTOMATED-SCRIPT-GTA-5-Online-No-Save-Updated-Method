# -AUTOMATED-SCRIPT-GTA-5-Online-No-Save-Updated-Method
This is an AHK script that will run the NoSave method of Force saving your online character and switching from Story mode to online.

You need to download Autohotkey ([https://www.autohotkey.com/](url))
Once installed, download the script and run it as an administrator.
For the Force save script to work: 
1. You need to have at least 2 outfits in your appearance for your online character.
2. Make sure in keybinds that your pause menu is on P.
3. Depending on the loading times of your game, you might have to change the timings on how long the script waits for story mode/Online loads.

This Script also has a manual NoSave on and off feature: 
* **Numpad 2**: Enable NoSave
* **Numpad 3**: Disable NoSave

Right before you finish a heist, press Numpad 2 to enable NoSave, then let the cutscene play out. It should say saving failed and couldn't connect to Rockstar servers. When the cutscene is over, and you get control of your online character again, press Numpad 1. This runs the automated script of taking you to story mode, disabling no save automatically, then going back into online, changing your outfit to force save your game, going back into story mode, then back into online, then you're ready to do the same heist again.
